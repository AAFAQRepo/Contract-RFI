# Script for AAFAQ_TEXT Style (Workflow Oriented)

Let me walk you through the problem this system solves and how AI changes the entire legal workflow.

In construction and enterprise projects, contracts and RFIs (Requests for Information) contain all critical legal and operational obligations—from payment terms and penalties to technical specifications and liability.

Today, this information is handled manually. Teams open each 100-page document, read the content line by line, and track obligations in spreadsheets. 

This manual process creates a massive operational bottleneck. It slows down project kick-offs, introduces human errors in risk assessment, and fails to scale as the number of subcontractors and vendors increases.

---

With this system, we use AI to eliminate that manual dependency.

Instead of manual contract review, users simply upload legal documents or RFIs into the platform, and the AI system automatically processes and understands the text, even in complex multilingual formats like Arabic, Hindi, and English.

---

From a user perspective, the workflow is simple.

Users log into a secure workspace and access a dashboard with three modules: Upload, Contract Chat, and Risk Review.

In the Upload module, users drag and drop PDF or Word documents. As soon as a file is uploaded, the AI ingestion pipeline starts. The system uses a unified parsing engine to understand layouts, tables, and clauses in real time.

---

Once processing is complete, the document is available in the Data Records module, which acts as a centralized, AI-powered repository for all project legal intelligence.

Each record includes metadata such as parties involved, dates, and processing status, along with full search and retrieval capability.

---

When a user opens a record, the AI has already extracted and structured all relevant legal information.

This includes penalty clauses, liability limits, termination rights, and payment milestones. 

Instead of manual interpretation, the AI provides a "Contract Chat" interface. You can ask, "What is the penalty for a 10-day delay?" and the AI returns the exact answer with direct citations to the clause and page number.

---

From a technical standpoint, the system uses a production-grade Hybrid RAG pipeline. 

It combines dense vector search for semantic meaning with keyword search for precision. The AI identifies risks, scores them by severity, and flags missing clauses that should be there based on industry standards.

Each processed document is stored with full traceability, including a unique ID, audit history, and original file preservation for legal compliance.

---

In summary, this AI system removes the critical manual bottleneck in contract management and RFI tracking.

With AI, what used to take days of legal review is now completed in seconds.
