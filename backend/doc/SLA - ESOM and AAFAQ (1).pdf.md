## &lt;b&gt;Agreement Overview&lt;/b&gt;

This Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of 01-Jan-2022 between EL SEIF FOR OPERATION &amp; MAINTENANCE (ESOM) (the Customer) and AAFAQ Advanced Solutions the service provider.

This Agreement represents a Service Level Agreement ("SLA" or "Agreement for Maintaining the IT services and modernize IT infrastructure and applications required to support and sustain ESOM business operations.

## 1. Periodic Review

This Agreement is valid from the Effective Date outlined herein and is valid for 2 years. This Agreement should be reviewed at a minimum once per fiscal year;

AAFAQ GM is responsible for facilitating regular reviews of this document. Contents of this document may be amended as required, provided mutual agreement is obtained from the stakeholders and communicated to all affected parties. The Document Owner will incorporate all subsequent revisions and obtain mutual agreements / approvals as required.

Next Review Date: 31-12-2023

## 2. Service Agreement

The following detailed service parameters are the responsibility of the Service Provider in the ongoing support of this Agreement. ALL custom Extension included in the following scope will be provided without extra cost and ESOM has the right to use them permanently.

## 2.1. Service Scope

The following Services are covered by this Agreement;

- Management of IT Department.
- IT Processes and Polices development.
- Prepare IT strategy and IT budget.
- Project management of the IT and ERP initiates and ensure that they deliver the business value.
- Providing IT and ERP staff to support ESOM operations.
- Business requirement documentation, software selections, approval of technical specification of HW.
- Enable ESOM to be License compliance and raise the recommendation to management for approval.

## Oracle ERP

- CRM and E-Invoicing)
- Development of Dashboards and Reports
- Access Role Management
- Security Management
- ERP End users support for ESOM, MES, MESI, ACC, Khaber Al-Insha, and EAA
